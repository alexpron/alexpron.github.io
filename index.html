Biography

I did my PhD thesis in Computer Science applied to Neurosciences under the supervision of Olivier Coulon and Christine Deruelle at the Institut de Neurosciences de la Timone (INT). I investigated the in vivo reconstruction and characterization of short association fibres of the human brain from diffusion-weighted Magnetic Resonance Imaging scans.
